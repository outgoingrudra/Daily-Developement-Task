The Morning Bird

        Golden rays kiss morning dew,
        Turning gray skies into blue.
        Birdsong weaves a  melody,
        Echoing through every tree.
        Rivers hum their ancient tune,
        Guided softly by the moon.
        Blossoms sway in tender grace,
        Nature’s smile on every face.
        In every breath, in every view—
        Beauty wakes the world anew.
          
poet : Rudra Verma
